Net privacy and freedom of speech


I think net privacy is a term most people do not understand. I've certainly learned a lot about the current lack of privacy
and how much information some of the largest companies on the planet have about me. Apple, Google and Facebook probably know
more about me than anyone of my parents or close friends, and if I was a dissident in an authoritarian regime, that might be
a huge problem.
George Orwell was closer to the mark than he had any reason to suspect. Technology is awesome but also
carries with it some very real problems. Problems that most people don't know about or ignore.
I think we should think more about these issues, and I am looking forward to discussing them in class.
